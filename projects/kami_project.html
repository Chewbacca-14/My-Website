Commercial App

Kami

Android & IOS

The KAMI app (soon to be rebranded as APAR) was developed for the eponymous manufacturer of eco-friendly electric trucks to facilitate freight transportation. The application enables users to order freight transportation by selecting points on the map, with the option to choose up to 3 delivery points. Users can view a list of all orders directly within the app, along with information about each order and route details.

Company operators managed this through an admin application, while orders were received through a Telegram channel. Unfortunately, the auto manufacturer recently went bankrupt and is no longer involved in vehicle production or freight transportation.

Main Technologies

  • Flutter
  • Dart
  • AppWrite
  • Google Maps

What I Did
