NullEnd Browser

Android & IOS

The application is a secure browser with a built-in VPN. It offers two modes of operation - Standard and Incognito. In Incognito mode, browsing history is not saved, cookies and cache are constantly cleared, and WebRTC is disabled. The app helps prevent accidental downloading of files or viruses to your device by requiring confirmation before initiating any downloads.

Additionally, it provides standard browser features such as tab browsing, bookmarking with editing capabilities, search history saving, and download tracking. The app is available in two languages - Russian and English. It boasts its own VPN (NullEnd VPN) seamlessly integrated into the application.

Main Technologies

  • Flutter
  • Dart
  • WebView
  • Firebase
